Support
Account
On the login screen, tap Forgot Password and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link — tap it and follow the instructions to set a new password.
Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm before anything is removed. Deleting your account permanently removes all your data, including your library, folders, ratings, and profile. This cannot be undone.
Go to Profile → Settings and tap the username field to edit it. Usernames must be unique — you'll be notified if your chosen name is already taken.
Library & Tracking
Tap the Search tab and find the anime you want. Open its detail page and tap the watch status button — choosing any status (Watching, Completed, Planned, Dropped, or Paused) adds it to your library.
Go to the Library tab and scroll down to the My Folders section. Tap New Folder, give it a name and a color, and it's ready to use. You can add anime to folders from the anime's detail page or by dragging them in the library view.
Watching — currently airing or in progress.
Completed — finished watching.
Planned — on your watchlist for later.
Paused — started but taking a break.
Dropped — started but stopped watching.
Social
Go to Profile → Friends and tap the Find tab. Search by username and send a friend request. Once they accept, you'll appear in each other's friend lists and can share folders with each other.
Open the folder you want to share and tap the share icon in the top right. You can share with any of your friends and choose whether they can view or edit the folder. They'll receive a notification and the folder will appear in their Library under Shared with Me.
Visit the user's profile and tap the ··· menu in the top right. From there you can block them (which prevents them from seeing your profile or sending requests) or submit a report. Reports are reviewed by our team.
General
Yumeka uses the AniList API as its anime database. AniList is a community-maintained database covering thousands of anime titles with detailed metadata, cover art, and more.
